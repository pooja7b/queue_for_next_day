This tool is developed as per the new Queue Management system proposed by Chakshu Piplani.

It will give the queue for next day based on the present day last queue.

Follow the below steps:

  1. Click on Browse and select the wordpad (.docx) file having the last queue of present day.
  2. Then enter the baseline value in the text box and click on Submit.
